SWEET LASSI

If you are looking for a cooling summer drink, then look no further than a glass of Lassi. Most popular in northers parts of India, lassi is a yogurt drink which can either be sweet or salty. Lassi is a yogurt drink especially popular in norther parts of India. It's super simple to make, so simple that you actually need only yogurt and water to make it.

Method to Prepare

INGREDIENTS

For Sweet Lassi :
  • Yogurt: Use plain, whole milk yogurt.
  • Ice cubes: Serves to properly chill the lassi and slightly dilute the yogurt.
  • Milk: Thins it out while adding richness and balance.
  • Heavy whipping cream: Amplifies the creamy base (a must for a good lassi) and enhances smoothness.
  • Sugar: You can make it as sweet or unsweet as you would like.

INSTRUCTIONS

Since we were using plenty of ice, a powerful blender helps. For regular blenders, you may have to partly crush the ice with the milk first.

  • Add ingredients in a blender and blend until no longer icy. If you would like to keep some of the bits of ice, blend for a shorter time.
  • It will naturally become frothy on top.
  • Taste and add sugar for sweetness, yogurt for more tart flavor, ice to make it cooler.
  • Pour into two large glasses or 3-4 small. This recipe makes around 4 cups (32 oz), so 2 large lassis or 4 kid-size, though my kids usually demand more than 8 oz.

Tips

  • Foamy & frothy: Before blenders were a thing, lassi was poured from one jug to another to create the signature froth. Thanks to blenders, now you can get the same froth sans arm workout.
  • Creamy & Smooth: My secret ingredient is a touch of whipping cream, which made the Lassi rich and silky without being heavy.
  • Cold: Temperature is one of the most overlooked aspects of a good Lassi, which is why ice is a required ingredient.
